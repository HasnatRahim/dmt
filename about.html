About Us

DEEN MARINE TRADE has rapidly evolved from traditional chandlery to a forward-thinking leader in Bangladesh's marine supply industry. With a focus on offering cost-efficient solutions for modern ship operators, we have built a reputation for service excellence and innovation.

Our extensive inventory includes over 45,000 marine store items, supported by a dedicated team of qualified professionals and advanced technology. This allows us to deliver market-leading service, consistently meeting the diverse needs of our clients. From food provisions to ship spare parts, mooring ropes, bunkering, marine lube oil, and specialized repair services, we provide a comprehensive range of products and services, maintaining high standards of quality and efficiency.

With roots in a long-established family marine trading business, we have grown to become the top importer and supplier of marine stores in Bangladesh, India, and Singapore. Over the last seven years, we have earned a first-class reputation for supplying high-quality marine equipment to both domestic and international clients.

As an ISO 9001:2015 certified company, DEEN MARINE TRADE ensures the highest standards in all aspects of our business. We are proud to be the trusted supplier for many leading fleet owners globally. Operating 24/7, 365 days a year, we deliver to all major ports in Bangladesh, including Payra Port and Mongla Port, and offer worldwide delivery services at competitive rates.

Founded in 2018, we continue to innovate and set new benchmarks in the marine supply industry.
