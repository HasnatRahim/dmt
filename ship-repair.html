Ship Repair Services

At Deen Marine Trade, we specialize in delivering comprehensive ship repair and maintenance services, ensuring that vessels of all types and sizes operate efficiently and safely. With years of expertise in the maritime industry, we provide high-quality, reliable solutions that minimize downtime and extend the life of your vessel.

Our Services Include:

Engine Overhauls

Comprehensive inspection, repair, and rebuilding of engines to ensure optimal performance.

Motor Rewinding

Expert motor rewinding services for enhanced efficiency and longevity.

Hydraulic Equipment Repair

We repair hydraulic systems to ensure smooth and powerful vessel operations.

24/7 Emergency Services

At Deen Marine Trade, we understand the importance of minimizing downtime. That's why we provide 24/7 emergency repair services, responding rapidly to get your ship back on course as quickly as possible.

Commitment to Quality & Safety

We take pride in our commitment to delivering repairs that meet or exceed industry standards. Utilizing only the highest quality materials and cutting-edge equipment, we ensure all repairs are done with precision and care.
